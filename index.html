6 Hat Thinking combined with Art & Making Prompts

I've been playing with Edward de Bono's 6 Thinking Hat system and Eno / Schmidt's Oblique Strategies and adapting / combining them. The content in each box is a quote, phrase or question I've personally found useful as encouragement or inspiration, or as a way of analysing the work or process. It's still a work in progress, but I've found applying the colours as 'thinking filters' and using the randomisation (refresh the page) useful strategies. I tend to have no problem in generating ideas, but find this helpful as either as a way of focusing or moving the work forward. Perhaps you do to?
